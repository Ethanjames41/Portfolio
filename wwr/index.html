I'm Ethan James. I build clean websites and improve them one project at a time.

Site Introduction: The home page of Ethan Portfolio explains what visitors can expect to find and why the site exists. It serves as a professional introduction for anyone who wants a quick, organized look at my work as a developing web designer and programmer. Instead of making a visitor search through class portals, file folders, or separate project links, the home page brings everything into one place. It introduces my focus on clean design, practical problem solving, and steady improvement as I build skills in HTML, CSS, layout, accessibility, and project organization.

Visitor Questions: People who arrive on the site will usually want answers to a few simple questions right away: who is Ethan James, what kind of work does he do, and where should I click next? The home page will answer those questions with a short introduction, a preview of my strongest skills, and clear links to the About Me and Projects pages. That structure matters because my audience includes instructors, classmates, internship coordinators, and employers who may only spend a minute or two on the site before deciding whether they want to keep reading.
