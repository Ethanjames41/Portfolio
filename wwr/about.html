About Ethan

Background: The About Me page gives visitors a fuller picture of who I am beyond a single headline. It will explain my current studies, the kinds of assignments and projects that have helped me grow, and the habits that shape how I work. This page is important because a portfolio is stronger when it includes the person behind the projects. Visitors should leave this page with a sense of my reliability, curiosity, and willingness to learn through practice, revision, and feedback.

Skills and Work Style: I plan to use this section to describe the technical and personal strengths that support my work. That includes writing semantic HTML, organizing CSS carefully, following instructions closely, and improving projects through testing and refinement. I also want this page to show that I value communication and consistency, because those qualities matter in both school and professional settings. Visitors who read this page should understand that I am still growing, but that I am serious about building useful, polished work.

  • Semantic HTML
  • Consistency
  • Revision
  • Communication

Growth Timeline

Goals: Another purpose of the About Me page is to show direction. I want visitors to know that this site is not only a record of what I have already completed, but also a place that will continue to grow as I add stronger projects and deeper skills. By including my goals, interests, and development process, the page can help employers or instructors understand the kind of opportunities I am preparing for.

  • Coursework Building stronger habits in HTML, CSS, layout, accessibility, and project organization through class assignments.
  • Practice Using planning, testing, and feedback to revise work until the structure and presentation feel polished.
  • Goals Continuing to add stronger projects and deeper technical skills as this portfolio grows.
